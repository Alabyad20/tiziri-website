Made to Order

Browse our designs and commission your perfect rug — woven to your exact size and color.

Not every piece we love is in stock. Our made-to-order service lets you choose from a growing catalogue of designs — Beni Ourain diamond grids, Amazigh tribal motifs, contemporary color combinations — and we'll work with our weavers in Morocco to make it exactly as you want it. Every rug is still handwoven by Berber artisans using traditional techniques. The only difference is it's made for you.

How it works

01

Choose a design

Browse the catalogue below. Pick the style, color palette and pattern that feels right. Mix ideas freely — we can adapt any design to your preference.

02

Tell us your size

Get in touch using the enquire button on each design. Tell us your dimensions and any adjustments to color or motif. We'll confirm pricing and lead time.

03

Your rug is woven

We commission the weaving with our Berber artisan partners in the Atlas region. Once complete, your rug ships directly from Morocco to your door — no extra leg, no delay. Delivered in 6–8 weeks from deposit.

Prefer a guided walkthrough? Try the Custom Rug Design Wizard.
