Made to Order

Browse our designs and commission your perfect rug — woven to your exact size and colour.

Not every piece we love is in stock. Our made-to-order service lets you choose from a growing catalogue of designs — Beni Ourain diamond grids, Amazigh tribal motifs, contemporary colour combinations — and we'll work with our weavers in Morocco to make it exactly as you want it. Every rug is still handwoven by Berber artisans using traditional techniques. The only difference is it's made for you.

How it works

01

Choose a design

Browse the catalogue below. Pick the style, colour palette and pattern that feels right. Mix ideas freely — we can adapt any design to your preference.

02

Tell us your size

Get in touch using the enquire button on each design. Tell us your dimensions and any adjustments to colour or motif. We'll confirm pricing and lead time.

03

Your rug is woven

We commission the weaving with our Berber artisan partners in the Atlas region. Your rug arrives in 6–8 weeks, delivered worldwide.

Design Catalogue

New designs added regularly. All pieces woven in 100% natural wool.
