Designer & Trade Program

For interior designers, architects, and hospitality projects.

Who it's for

Built for working professionals.

TIZIRI works with interior designers, architects, and hospitality projects sourcing handwoven Moroccan rugs — whether for a single client piece or an ongoing series of projects. Our team can assist with product selection, sizing, and production timelines for made-to-order pieces.

What you get

Exclusive trade pricing.

Approved trade accounts receive exclusive pricing on every piece in the catalogue and on custom made-to-order commissions — confirmed once your account is set up.

0 Minimum order — apply and start ordering at any project size.
1 Application, one form. Tell us about your studio and we'll set your account up.

Trade pricing applies to every rug in the catalogue and to custom made-to-order commissions. There's no annual fee and no required volume — it's built for designers who order once a year and designers who order every month alike.

How it works

Apply, then order.

Send us your studio or business name, a link to your portfolio or website, and the type of project you're sourcing for. We reply within one working day to confirm your trade account and talk through your first order.
