Chairish professional-seller application

Step 1 (the email — one click): Open the application email to sales@chairish.com in Gmail, review, hit Send.

Step 2 (account — Abdel only, ~5 min): go to chairish.com → Join → create the account with hello@tizirirugs.com as "Tiziri Rugs". No vetting to create it — you start on the free Consignor plan and are auto-upgraded to Professional at 10 live listings.

Step 3 (first listings, after the account exists): start with the 5 strongest photographed pieces (suggest: 1 ivory Beni Ourain, 2 Boujaad, 1 Mrirt, 1 kilim). Every listing goes through Chairish curation review — lead photo on a clean background, honest condition notes, real dimensions. Price the SAME as the site (cross-channel rule #3), let Chairish's commission come out of margin.

Remember (cross-channel rule #1): one-of-one inventory — when a rug sells on Chairish, delist it everywhere else the same day, and vice versa.

Why we're doing this: Chairish is the stepping stone to 1stDibs (see tiziri channel map, phase-2 marketplaces, decided 2026-07-09).
