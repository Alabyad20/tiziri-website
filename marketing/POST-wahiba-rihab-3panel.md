## Why Rihab goes first

Clip `195323` turned out to be **hands running through the pile** — already shot, back on 11 July. That was the single highest-leverage change I flagged after pulling the insights: 962 reach produced 1 save and 0 shares because product pans give a scroller nothing human to land on. Faces and hands consistently outperform objects.

So Rihab's bottom panel is 30 seconds of continuous hands-on-wool, and the caption leads on touch rather than pattern. If the format is going to work, this is the post that shows it.

**Suggested order:** Rihab → (3 days) → Wahiba. Different rug, different palette, different hook — no repetition, and it gives you a clean read on whether hands-in-frame beats pattern-only.

---

## What to watch

Baseline across the 19 posts before these: **median reach ~44, best 119, 1 save total, 0 comments ever.**

Judge on **saves and comments, not reach.** Reach was never the problem — Instagram was already showing the reels to ~100 strangers each. The question is whether anyone cares once they see them. More than 2 saves, or any comment at all, means the format earns its keep.
