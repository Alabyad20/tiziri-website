# How to pin the three 3-panel videos

All three files are in **`C:\Users\nigel\Desktop\TIZIRI-SPLIT-TEST\`** and all are within Pinterest's limits (mp4/H.264, 1080×1920, 13–21 MB, 29–30s — Pinterest allows 4s to 15min, up to 2GB).

Do this in the browser as **Tiziri Rugs**. It cannot be scripted: `POST /pins` is blocked on Trial access, and browser automation can't reach local files.

---

## The steps (same for each video)

1. **pinterest.com** → top-left **Create** → **Create Pin**
2. **Drag the .mp4 onto the upload area.** Wait for it to finish processing — the preview will start playing. Don't fill anything in until it does; the form can drop fields mid-upload.
3. **Title** — paste from the table below. ⚠ **This is the field that decides whether the Pin is ever found.** Pinterest is a search engine and the title is its strongest ranking signal. Every one of your existing 17 Buffer pins has an empty title, which is why they rank for nothing.
4. **Description** — paste from below.
5. **Destination link** — paste the product URL. ⚠ **The single most important field.** Without it the Pin goes nowhere, and a URL typed into the description is *not* clickable.
6. **Board** — pick the one named below.
7. **Publish** — or click the arrow next to it to **schedule** (free, up to 30 days out).

Repeat three times. About 3 minutes each.

## Notes

- **No hashtags.** Pinterest deprioritised them years ago and they read as spam. The keywords belong in the title and description, which is how these are written.
- **A pin's image/video can never be changed** after publishing — title, description and link can. So get the video right first (these are final), and don't worry about the text.
- **Re-pin later, don't duplicate now.** In a few weeks, save each Pin to a second relevant board (`Moroccan Rugs`, `Earthy Boho Interiors`, `Neutral Living Rooms`). Fresh saves to different boards keep earning impressions without creating duplicate Pins.
- These will be your **first video pins with proper titles and working destination links** — worth checking their performance against the untitled Buffer ones in a couple of weeks.
