# Pinterest — paste-ready descriptions

**Why these differ from the Instagram captions:** Pinterest is a *search engine*. Pins rank on keywords in the description, so these front-load the terms buyers actually type — style, colour, size in feet, room — rather than mood copy. Hashtags are NOT used; Pinterest deprioritised them and they read as spam.

**How to use:** Pinterest → Create Pin → upload the image or video → paste **Title** and **Description** → set **Destination link** → choose the **Board** → *Publish at a later date* to schedule (free, up to 30 days out).

**Pin each rug to its primary board now, and re-pin to the secondary board a few weeks later** — fresh pins to different boards keep earning new impressions.
