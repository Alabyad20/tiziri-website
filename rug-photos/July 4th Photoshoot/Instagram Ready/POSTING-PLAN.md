## Step 0 — Profile setup (once, before Day 1)

- **Name field:** TIZIRI
- **Username:** @tizirirugs
- **Category:** Home decor
- **Bio:**
  > Berber rugs sourced directly from weavers in Morocco.
  > No wholesalers. No duplicates.
  > tizirirugs.com
- **Link:** https://tizirirugs.com
- Switch to a **Business account** (free) so you get insights and can add the Shop tab later.
- Profile photo: use the TIZIRI logo from `rug-photos/July 4th Photoshoot/logos/`.

**Posting mechanics (applies to everything below):**
- Post between **6–8 pm ET** (evening scroll, US East Coast buyers).
- Add location tag **Boston, Massachusetts** on product posts — "ships from the US" is a selling point; the rugs really are here.
- After posting, **share each post to your Story** the same evening (one tap) — doubles the reach for free.
- For reels, pick a **cover frame** where the whole rug is visible (Instagram asks when you post).
- Reply to every comment in the first hour if you can — the algorithm rewards it.
