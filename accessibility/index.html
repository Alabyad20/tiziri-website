TIZIRI / Accessibility Statement

Accessibility Statement

Plain English.

Our commitment

Accessibility at TIZIRI.

We want everyone to be able to browse and buy from this site, regardless of ability or the technology they use to access it. We are working toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 at Level AA.

Current measures

What we've done.

Steps already in place across the site:

  • A "Skip to content" link on every page for keyboard users
  • A visible keyboard focus indicator on links, buttons, and form fields
  • Semantic landmark structure (a single main content region per page) for screen reader navigation
  • Descriptive alt text on product photography
  • Proper heading structure on product and content pages
  • Labeled form fields and accessible names on icon-only buttons
  • Contact form errors are announced inline, next to the field that needs attention, and keyboard focus moves there automatically
  • The mobile menu and the promotional pop-up keep keyboard focus contained while open, and return it to where you were when closed
  • The Custom Rug Design Wizard announces each step and your selections to screen readers as you move through it
  • Secondary text (section labels, captions, and similar smaller copy) meets the WCAG AA contrast ratio against its background
Ongoing work

Known limitations.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time fix. We have not yet completed a full manual screen reader pass across every page — our review so far has focused on the contact form, main navigation, pop-ups, and the interactive rug tools. Some older content may not fully meet every guideline. We are continuing to review and improve the site over time.

Report an issue

Get in touch.

If you encounter an accessibility barrier anywhere on this site, please tell us — the page, what happened, and the device or assistive technology you were using. Email hello@tizirirugs.com and we will do our best to address it promptly.

Changes to this statement

Updates.

We will update this page as we make further accessibility improvements.
