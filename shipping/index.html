TIZIRI / Shipping & Returns

Shipping & Returns

Handled with care.

Getting it to you

Delivery.

Each rug is wrapped and dispatched individually — there is no warehouse, no batch shipping. Every piece leaves rolled in protective cloth and travels to you via a tracked courier service. You will receive a tracking link by email within 24 hours of dispatch.

United Kingdom 3–5 working days
Europe 4–7 working days
Rest of world 7–14 working days

Shipping is complimentary on all orders.

If it isn't right

Returns.

We understand that buying a rug without standing on it is an act of trust. If a piece does not work in your space, return it within 14 days of delivery and we will collect it — no questions asked.

Return window 14 days from delivery
Cost Complimentary collection
Condition Unrolled for inspection only — no signs of use or wear
Refund Processed within 5 working days of collection

To arrange a return, email us at abdelkebirlabyad@gmail.com. We will organise collection at a time that suits you.

If you have doubts before buying — about size, pile height, or how a colour reads in a specific light — write to us first. We can send additional photographs or a short video of any piece before you commit.

Anything else

Questions.

If something isn't covered here, get in touch. We reply within one working day.
