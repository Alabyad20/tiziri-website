TIZIRI / Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Plain English.

The data controller

Who we are.

TIZIRI is a curated Moroccan rug shop operated by Abdel Kebir. When we collect your personal data, we act as the data controller — meaning we decide what is collected and why.

You can reach us with any questions about your data at hello@tizirirugs.com. We will respond within one month.

Personal data

What we collect.

We collect the minimum needed to run a small shop. Here is what that means in practice:

Newsletter sign-up Your email address, collected when you subscribe. Used only to send you updates about new arrivals and occasional news from TIZIRI.
Contact & product enquiries Your name, email address, and message. These are sent through your own email client directly to us — we receive them as standard email and use them only to reply to you.
Email correspondence If you email us directly, we hold that conversation in our email account for as long as it is relevant to our relationship.

We do not collect payment data. We do not run a server that stores form submissions. We do not build profiles.

Purpose and legal basis

How we use it.

Sending newsletter emails Consent — you opted in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send.
Responding to your enquiry Legitimate interests — you contacted us and expect a reply.
Business correspondence Legitimate interests — keeping normal records of our communications.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

External services

Third parties.

Running this site requires a small number of third-party services. Each processes data under their own privacy policy.

Google Fonts This site loads typefaces from Google's servers. When your browser requests them, Google may log your IP address. See Google's privacy policy.
Newsletter platform If you subscribe, your email address is stored with a third-party email marketing service. We will name that service and link to its privacy policy once it is in place. You will not be subscribed without your explicit consent.
Analytics We do not currently use analytics. If we add analytics in the future, we will update this page before enabling it.
Hosting This site is hosted on a third-party static hosting platform. Standard server logs (IP address, pages visited, timestamps) may be retained by the host for security purposes.
Browser storage

Cookies.

We do not set tracking or advertising cookies on this site. We do not use cookies to follow you across the web or build an advertising profile.

Google Fonts may set a cookie as part of its delivery service. This is a functional cookie that does not track you across other sites.

If we add analytics in the future, we will introduce a cookie notice and obtain your consent before placing any tracking cookies.

How long we keep it

Data retention.

Newsletter subscribers Until you unsubscribe. Once you unsubscribe, your email is removed from our mailing list.
Email correspondence For as long as it is relevant to our business relationship — typically the duration of a purchase enquiry and any follow-up. We delete correspondence that no longer serves a purpose.
UK & EU GDPR

Your rights.

Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data where there is no longer a lawful reason to hold it
  • Restriction — ask us to pause processing while a dispute is resolved
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing carried out on the basis of legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time; this does not affect any processing already carried out

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tizirirugs.com. We will respond within one month.

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. In the UK this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO). If you are in the EU, contact your national data protection authority.

Changes to this policy

Updates.

We will update this page when our practices change — for example, when we add analytics or name our newsletter platform. The date below reflects the last revision.
